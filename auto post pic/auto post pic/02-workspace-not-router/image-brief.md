# Image Brief: Workspace Not Router

**Content Set:** 02
**Format:** Carousel (5 slides)

---

## Target Audience

Technical users who have tried model routers (OpenRouter, Poe, LiteLLM) and found them insufficient. Developers, power users, and AI early adopters who switch between tools frequently and are frustrated by context loss.

## Language

English

## Tone

Direct. Technical but accessible. Slightly provocative — challenges the assumption that model routing is the answer. No hype. No buzzwords. Confident.

## Core Message

Model routers solved access. They did not solve continuity. Your workspace should follow you — not reset every time you switch tools.

---

## Carousel Structure (5 Slides)

### Slide 1

**Headline:** You Don't Need Another Model Router
**Subtext:** You need your workspace to follow you.
**Visual:** A clean split screen. Left side: a tangled web of arrows connecting dozens of model logos (representing router complexity). Right side: a single sleek device with a clean beam of light connecting it to a laptop, a desktop, and a monitor — simple, linear, one device to many screens.

### Slide 2

**Headline:** 200+ Models. Same Broken Workflow.
**Subtext:** Model routers solved access. Your context still dies when you close the tab.
**Visual:** A long scrolling list of model names (blurred slightly to suggest "too many to matter") with a red "X" over a session timeline that cuts off abruptly. A visual metaphor for context loss — a dotted line that stops mid-path.

### Slide 3

**Headline:** The Real Problem Is Not Which Model
**Subtext:** It is where your workspace lives.
**Visual:** A question mark inside a cloud icon with a crack through it. Below it, a solid drive icon with a checkmark. The contrast is between uncertain cloud dependency and physical certainty.

### Slide 4

**Headline:** Your AI Assistant. In Your Pocket.
**Subtext:** Files, memory, context — all on one drive, all on every machine.
**Visual:** A top-down view of a sleek drive plugged into a laptop. Concentric rings radiate outward, labeled "Files," "Memory," "Context," "Sessions" — representing the layers of workspace that travel with the device. OpenClaw branding subtly visible on the drive.

### Slide 5

**Headline:** Plug In. Your AI Remembers Everything.
**Subtext:** OpenClaw — your AI assistant, always ready, anywhere you work.
**Visual:** A hand holding the drive, plugging it into a new laptop. On the screen, the exact same workspace, same open files, same AI session — a visual continuation from wherever the user last stopped. The drive glows softly, like it's alive. Clean, minimal, product-focused.

## Words to Avoid

- Revolutionize
- Game-changer
- Next-gen
- Seamless
- Cutting-edge
- Powered by
- Leverage
- Synergy
- Unlock
- Empower
- Lobster (use OpenClaw)
- USB drive (use "drive" or "device")

## Visual Copy Rules

- No emoji in image text
- Headlines: maximum 8 words
- Subtext: maximum 14 words
- Use sentence case, not title case
- High contrast text on clean backgrounds
- One idea per slide — do not overload
- Brand: OpenClaw (not Lobster USB)
