## 14. FAQ 文案片段

### Is this a local AI model?

MORA Drive is a local-first AI workspace. Your workspace, prompts, history and indexes live on the drive. Advanced reasoning uses the MORA model gateway unless local mode is enabled.

### Can I use it on any computer?

You can use Web Mode from any trusted browser. Workspace Mode is designed for computers you trust and where running a signed portable app is allowed.

### What happens if I lose the drive?

Your local workspace is encrypted. You can revoke the drive from your account and recover your workspace if encrypted backup is enabled.

### Is it just an AI API reseller?

No. MORA includes a model gateway, but the product is a portable private AI workspace: prompts, memory, files, project context, credits and AI tools in one place.
